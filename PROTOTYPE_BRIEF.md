# Speak With Confidence — prototype brief

## Source insight
Users want help turning exposure into confident speaking, not just passive recognition.

## Product bet
After a lesson, Memrise should not only check whether the learner recognized the phrase. It should help them feel ready to say it in a plausible real-world moment.

## What this prototype explores
A mobile-first post-lesson speaking coach that:
- starts from native audio
- invites a low-stakes spoken attempt
- gives gentle, specific feedback
- asks for one realistic response without full sentence scaffolding
- holds progression until speaking confidence is good enough

## Main screen modules
1. Readiness hero
2. Confidence check by skill type
3. 1-minute confidence loop: hear it → say it → use it
4. Targeted weak-phrase blocker card
5. Advance controls based on readiness

## Why this is interesting
The prototype shifts the product promise from “I got it right in the app” to “I could actually say this out loud when it matters.”

## Cautions
- Confidence score is a product concept, not yet a validated metric.
- Feedback should feel supportive, not punitive or overly evaluative.
- The value depends on good native audio and genuinely useful speaking feedback.
