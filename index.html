High-fidelity mobile concept: Practice until I’m ready

A post-lesson checkpoint that reduces premature progression by giving learners explicit control to repeat, target weak spots, or continue when they actually feel fluent enough.

Biggest blocker

The app moves on before the learner feels ready.

That creates fragile progress. The user wants confidence, not just completion.

UX improvement

Add a deliberate checkpoint after lesson completion.

  • Repeat lesson
  • Practice difficult words
  • Continue to next lesson
  • Optional auto-advance preference

Design intent

Why this concept is stronger than a generic completion screen

  • Reframes success: readiness, not just completion.
  • Supports hesitant speakers: lets them repeat safely before progressing.
  • Makes weakness actionable: identifies exactly what to practice next.
  • Preserves momentum: users can still continue with one tap.
  • Creates a product lever: auto-advance can be personalized later.

Suggested MVP test

Show this checkpoint only when speaking confidence or pronunciation drops below threshold, then measure repeat usage, lesson completion, and 7-day return rate.
