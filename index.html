Strongest JTBD

Belong and function confidently in France / Europe

When I travel to France or imagine living in Europe, I want to understand and speak French in real situations, so I can participate in everyday life and feel part of the community, without constantly needing people to slow down or switch to English.

Why this looks strongest: it connects her aspiration, identity, travel behaviour, and desired real-world progress most clearly.

Top product signal

Flexibility beats forced progression

The sharpest comparative win vs Duolingo is not “learning languages” in general — it’s giving the learner control over how they practice when confidence and available time vary.
